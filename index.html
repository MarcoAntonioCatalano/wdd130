Marco A Catalano | WDD 130

I'm Marco Catalano, from Rosario, Argentina. I love architecture and design in general. I have a bachelors in Interior Design. In my free time I enjoy outdoor sports and traveling the most. Since I mention traveling, I'm actually visiting Argentina right now. A friend of mine that I've known since I was 5 years old got married on this past Saturday. What a wonderful fun moment was to celebrate a dear friend. Weddings have many different cultural protocols in different countries. Here in Argentina, right after the matrimonial ceremony it comes the dinning section, which is fairly equal to other weddings. But later on you have the dancing. The dancing could last until very early hours of the next day. For example, we stayed until 3:30 am. Traveling teaches me how diverse societies are, how important it is to respect and appreciate cultural views on social interactions. On another note, and also something that I enjoy is being outdoor. Exercise can be done many ways. While traveling I love to go on walks as part of my exercise. Because I can connected with sight seeing. There are many beautiful european building that date the post-world wars european migrations to the southern continent. Walking it's not just a great exercise, but it becomes fun, as I walk through renown architectural buildings. So, a win win situation. Last but no least, I'd like to mention another cultural exchange that enriches cultures, that's the culinary arts. Gastronomy in this case "Argentina," portrays a wide range of dishes from many european countries. There are so many communities that it would dificult to name them all, but the most well-know are, Spanish dishes from different regions of Spanish, Italy and its regions. Also, Germans, Polish, Russian, Middle eastern, etc.
